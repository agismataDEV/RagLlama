## 📋 About RagLlama

RagLlama lets you chat with your own documents without sending data online. It keeps everything on your computer to protect your privacy. You can ask questions or search through your files easily. The app uses React for the interface, Node.js for running the backend, PostgreSQL with pgvector for storing data, and Ollama for handling the language model.  

The app helps you find answers quickly by combining your documents with smart AI tools, all on your local machine.

## 🖥️ System Requirements

To use RagLlama on Windows, you need:

- Windows 10 or newer (64-bit recommended)  
- At least 4 GB of RAM (8 GB or more for larger data sets)  
- 2 GHz or faster processor  
- Minimum 500 MB free disk space (more may be needed for large document collections)  
- An internet connection for the initial download only, not during use

## 🛠️ Technical Overview (Optional)

If you want to understand more about what runs behind the scenes:  

- React builds the user interface you interact with.  
- Node.js runs the local server to process your questions and manage data flow.  
- PostgreSQL with the pgvector extension stores documents in a way the AI can search quickly.  
- Ollama acts as the language model engine that helps understand and answer your queries.  

All these parts work together on your computer without sending your data anywhere.
